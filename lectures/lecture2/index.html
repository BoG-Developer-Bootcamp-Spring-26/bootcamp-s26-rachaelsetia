Roles

Bits of Good is home to engineers and artists. We believe in empowering the non-profits and democratizing technology for social good.

Design

User-focused members that either work with nonprofits to solve their needs from a UX and UI perspective, or work with our brand team to illuminate the BoG mission.

Marketing

The communicators and creative writers that advertise our mission. They handle our social media, flyers, and our upcoming BoG Blog content.

Engineering

The bread and butter of our nonprofit teams. These members may work in any area of the stack to learn and deliver powerful web applications.

Product

The leaders that weigh the needs of our nonprofits, technical challenges of our devs, and creative vision of our designers to deliver production-ready applications.

Operations

The leaders of our events, finances, and logistics. This department is the glue finding fun, creative ways to bring our community closer together.

Brand

Artisan of online presence, print, and BoG "look and feel."

Nonprofit Partnership

Advocates ready to connect with potential nonprofits.
